Active Voice & Passive Voice

Introduction

Voice
-In grammer ,voice refers to the form of verb.
Voices are of following two types:-

Active voice
-The subject of a sentence performs the action is called Active voice.
In Active voice 'S-Subject+H-Helping verb+M-Main verb+O-Object(SHMO)' pattern is followed.
For example:-
  • Tom writes a letter.
  • Jerry tears the letter.etc.

Benefits of Active voice:-

  1. Take less time in formation.
  2. Easy to understand.
  3. Take less time to speak.

Passive Voice
-A verb is said to be in the Passive Voice when the subject of the verb is acted upon or receives the action.
In Passive Voice 'O-Object+H-Helping verb+M-Main verb+B-By+S-Subject(OHMBS)' pattern is followed.
For example:-
  • A letter is written by tom.
  • The letter is teared by jerry.

Benefits of Passive voice:-

  1. It upgrades the sentence.
  2. It increases the standard of speaker.
  3. Make sentence pithy.

Disadvantages of Passive Voice:-

  1. May lead to misunderstand.
  2. Difficult to understand.
  3. Take 25% more time in formation.

Active Voice changes in Passive Voice in the following condition if :-

  • The verb is transtive.

Active Voice does not change in Passive Voice in the following condition if :-

  • The verb is intranstive.
  • In the sentence has no object .
  • The sentence is in perfect continouos.
